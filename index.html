Eleanor Tursman

I am an emerging technologies researcher at the Aspen Institute working towards a more public AI ecosystem.

Previously, I served as a TechCongress Congressional Innovation Scholar working in the office of Representative Trahan on health tech, educational tech, and data privacy. Before that, I got my masters in computer science at Brown University working with James Tompkin. My research in computer vision focused on 3D vision, informed by my undergraduate degree in physics.
